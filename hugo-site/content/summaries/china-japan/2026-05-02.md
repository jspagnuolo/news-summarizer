- Japan is actively pursuing economic security through critical minerals partnerships, notably with Australia and Vietnam.
- China expresses concerns over Japan's military expansions and nuclear ambitions, framing them as a resurgence of militarism.
- Both nations are navigating complex geopolitical landscapes, with Japan focusing on economic interventions and China emphasizing historical narratives and regional stability.
- Japan's recent arms export policy changes are viewed critically by China, which perceives them as steps toward remilitarization.
- Economic ties and energy procurement strategies are central to both countries' current agendas.
