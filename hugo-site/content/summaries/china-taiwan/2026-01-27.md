- Tensions between China and Taiwan are escalating, with China criticizing Japan's PM for remarks on Taiwan, while Taiwan is enhancing its military capabilities with US support.
- China is actively pushing back against perceived foreign interference in Taiwan's affairs, particularly from Japan and the US.
- Taiwan is facing internal challenges, including espionage cases and delays in defense budget approvals, which may affect its military readiness.
- Both sides are engaged in a war of narratives, with China ridiculing Taiwan's military claims while Taiwan emphasizes its defense efforts against Chinese pressure.
