- Tensions between China and Taiwan continue to escalate, with both sides accusing each other of historical distortions and political manipulation.
- China criticizes Taiwan's leadership for perceived betrayal and historical inaccuracies, while Taiwan expresses concerns over China's actions amid international conflicts.
- Taiwan is wary of China's potential exploitation of the US-Iran war to bolster its own geopolitical stance.
- China emphasizes the need for peaceful reunification and infrastructure upgrades for Taiwan post-reunification, while Taiwan remains skeptical of such promises.
- International reactions, particularly from Japan and Lithuania, highlight the complex diplomatic landscape surrounding Taiwan's status.
