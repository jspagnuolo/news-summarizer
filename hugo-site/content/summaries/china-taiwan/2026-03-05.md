- China is open to dialogue but firmly opposes any moves towards Taiwan independence, viewing it as a dead end.
- Taiwan is critical of China's economic reforms and the implications for its own security and autonomy.
- Taiwan's media highlights the need for cautious engagement with China, stressing that benefits must outweigh potential risks.
