- Both China and Taiwan are reacting to each other's political moves, with China emphasizing sovereignty and Taiwan focusing on internal governance issues.

- China views Taiwan's impeachment of Lai as a sign of instability and internal conflict within Taiwan's government.

- Chinese media emphasizes military preparedness and the potential transformation of civilian vessels into military ones as a deterrent against perceived threats from Taiwan and the U.S.

- Taiwan is tightening travel rules for officials visiting China, reflecting a cautious approach to cross-strait relations and a desire to limit influence from Beijing.
- Local news outlets focus on domestic issues, such as childcare regulations, indicating a broader concern for governance beyond the China-Taiwan conflict.
